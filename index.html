About

WAX DOG SOUND LAB is a recording studio based in West Vancouver, BC. The studio features a large space that is sound proofed for recording and fitted with a wide variety of instruments and microphones for rent at an extra charge.

To book the studio or ask questions, please contact Dayton Bowen aka WAX DOG by emailing waxdogsoundlab@gmail.com or clicking the button below. Please include your name and what services you are interested in.

Gear Rental

Simple Mix, Edit, Clean
40$ flat rate, or on studio time with artists

Includes:

  • A basic simple live off the floor sounding mix
  • Compression, gates and EQ and leveling for cleaning purposes.
  • Does NOT include effects like reverb, delay, saturation, ect.
  • One revision mix upon delivery(addition revisions 15$ each)

Studio Notes

Delivery

I can deliver individual stems, all individual takes, the entire pro tools session, and/or a bounced mix. Mixing and mastering are not included in my services. I can do a simple mix for 40$(details listed above).

Payment

A down payment of 1 hour(30$) + 50% of instrument rentals is required. The remaining portion is to be payed at the end of the session. I accept cash, e-transfer, or card.

Location

If you intend to record multiple days in a week you're welcome to leave your gear at the studio. The property is secured with alarms and security cameras. There is also always someone present on the property.
